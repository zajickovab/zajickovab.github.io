Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we have ample parking available for our guests. There is a parking lot adjacent to the restaurant, and street parking is also available nearby. If you have any issues finding parking, please let us know, and we’ll be happy to assist you.

Yes, we offer catering services for all types of events, including corporate functions, weddings, and private parties. Our catering menu features a selection of our most popular dishes and can be customized to suit your event. Please contact our catering manager for more details and to discuss your specific needs.

Yes, we offer both takeout and delivery services. You can place your takeout order by calling us directly or ordering online through our website. For delivery, we partner with several local delivery services, which can be accessed via our website. Delivery areas and times may vary, so please check online or contact us for more information.

Yes, we take food allergies and dietary restrictions very seriously. Please inform your server of any allergies or restrictions you have, and our kitchen staff will take the necessary precautions to ensure your meal is prepared safely. We also offer gluten-free and low-sodium options.
